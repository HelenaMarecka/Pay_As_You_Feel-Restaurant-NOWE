The Cuboard Goods Box

Pay As You Feel Box

We're super excited to share another cool initiative we're rolling out. Thanks to our awesome connections, we're kicking off a "pay as you feel" food box program. What's in the box? Only the freshest, most delicious goodies rescued from local grocery stores and farmers markets. You can snag one from select pickup spots around the city on certain days. But here's the catch: it's a limited item, so you gotta hop online and reserve your box for the week. Why are we doing this? Simple. We want to make sure everyone in our community has access to nourishing food, especially those who are struggling. By promoting these boxes in community hubs, we're hoping to lend a hand to folks who need it most, all while respecting their dignity and autonomy.

Where You Can Find The Cuboard Goods Box

We believe everyone should have access to quality ingredients, regardless of their financial situation. That's why we're letting you decide how much you want to pay for your box. It's all about flexibility and affordability, without compromising on quality. So, if you're ready to elevate your cooking game with the freshest ingredients in town, sign up for our subscription box today. Let's make healthy eating accessible to everyone in our community!
